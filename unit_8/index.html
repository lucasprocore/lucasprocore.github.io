About

Frankenstein “Frankie” Holifield was born in a dog shelter in Simi Valley, California on October 1, 2017. Originally given the name “Winter” by the owners of the shelter, Frankie was born in a litter of seven (some pictured above), which was perfect for his playful disposition. Now, since I work in a dog-friendly office, Frankie gets to come to work with me every day! Growing up in a shelter was a great experience for him, as he learned how to interact with other dogs of all shapes and sizes. Check out our photos section for pictures of Frankie as a puppy, and with some work friends too!
