That’s Gonna Be a Reservoir From Me, Dog. — HARD 1

There’s a famous scene where the characters argue over the principles of tipping your waitress. Who is against tipping? Do they end up putting in their fair share?

Even though Mr. Pink feels that paying tips to waitresses who don't come for refills enough "is for the dogs", Mr. White argues that it is a main source of income for females who don't attend college. Joe makes him chip in his dollar toward the bill anyway.
